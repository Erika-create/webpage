    Current Problems

    Time and cost of drug discovery is extremely inefficient. The current cost spent on medical care every year is 6-7% of global domestic product, which is US$8.5-9 trillion.

    Cost Time Success Rate
    $1 billion 14 years 10% (3% for cancer)

    As the success rate is extremely low, investing becomes risky, resulting in the cost of accepted medications to increase to make up for the setbacks.

    Benefits of AI in Drug Discovery

    Development of AI have contributed the notable growth of medical information recently. Inclusion of AI could positively effect the drug discovery process:

    • Speeds up the drug discovery process by including AI for the medical data analysation
    • Failures could be prevented
    • Digitalisation of medical records could be benefitial in data-based methods
    • AI application in drug discovery includes:
      • De novo molecular design and optimisation
      • Drug design based on the structure
      • Clinical trials
